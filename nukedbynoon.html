Project · AI · Game Design

Nuked by Noon is a stealth game where I programmed all gameplay systems and collaborated with artists on the visual assets. I developed an enemy AI using Unreal Engine's State Trees and AI Perception, allowing the enemy to detect, search for, and attack the player intelligently.
